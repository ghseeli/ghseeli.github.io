George H. Seelinger



I am a Mathematics PhD student at the University of Virginia studying abstract algebra with a particular interest in representation theory and algebraic combinatorics; I am currently looking for research problems. Previously, I worked as a Data Scientist on real time bidding problems for an internet advertising company. Generally, I am interested in working on unsolved problems and enabling other problem solvers to be more effective.

Teaching
