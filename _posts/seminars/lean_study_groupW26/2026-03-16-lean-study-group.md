We briefly discussed people's progress on their projects. We then spent the remainder of the time "live coding" an approach to proving a general version of the [Brouwer fixed point theorem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brouwer_fixed-point_theorem). This involved a lot of trial-and-error as we practiced considering the details of how to actually formalize something.

More details, including the end result of the session, are in the [`lean_lec_mar16.lean`](https://github.com/ghseeli/michigan-lean-study-group/blob/master/lean_lectures/lean_lec_mar16.lean) file.
