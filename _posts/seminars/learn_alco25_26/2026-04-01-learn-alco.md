Building on the type A example from last week, we will explore Cambrian lattices for general Coxeter groups. For a coxeter element c, we will introduce c-sorting words and c-sortable elements. We will see how these can be used to describe interesting lattice congruences and connect the quotient lattices to generalized associahedra via c-clusters and c-noncrossing elements.
